Location

CHIJMES Main Hall

30 Victoria Street, Singapore 187996

A landmark of colonial grace and stained-glass grandeur, CHIJMES sets an incomparable stage for an evening to remember. Located in the heart of the Civic District, it is easily accessible by public transport.

The nearest MRT stations are City Hall (EW13/NS25) and Bras Basah (CC2), both within a short walk.

Frequently Asked

Everything else you might want to know before the evening.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive around 7:00 PM for guest arrival and snacks. The official welcome and march in will begin at 7:30 PM, followed by dinner at 8:00 PM.

Where is the celebration held?

The celebration will be held at CHIJMES Main Hall, 30 Victoria Street, Singapore 187996.

Is there a dress code?

Please come in cocktail attire. Our venue colours are white, champagne, baby pink, and pastel green, so guests are warmly welcome to dress in those tones.

Do I need to bring a gift?

Your presence is already a gift. If you would still like to bless the couple, a simple gift or red packet is warmly appreciated.

How do I confirm my attendance?

Please complete the RSVP form above to confirm your attendance. You may also join our WhatsApp group to stay updated on the latest details for the evening.
